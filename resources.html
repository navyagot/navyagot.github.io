Mental Health Awareness

Office on Women's Health

The resources on this page can help you find support, guidance, and help if you are concerned about your mental health or the mental health of someone you love.

National Institute of Mental Health

The National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH) is a federal agency that conducts research on mental disorders and aims to prevent and cure mental illness with a mission is to improve the understanding and treatment of mental illnesses through research and training scientists.

SAHMSA

SAMHSA's National Helpline is a free, confidential, 24/7, 365-day-a-year treatment referral and information service (in English and Spanish) for individuals and families facing mental and/or substance use disorders.

Importance:

Mental health is an important part of our overall well-being, yet it is often overlooked and stigmatized. Recognizing the need for help is a critical first step in promoting mental your mental health. Just as we seek medical attention for physical health, it is vital to address emotional and psychological challenges with the same urgency.It is essential to remember that seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness. If you or someone you know is struggling, reach out for support. Mental health matters, and you deserve to live a healthy, happy life.
